ESP32-CAM Viewer

Enter your ESP32-CAM's IP address to view the stream

How to find the IP address:

  1. Connect your ESP32-CAM to your computer
  2. Open Serial Monitor in Arduino IDE
  3. Look for a message like: "Camera Stream URL: http://192.168.x.x/stream"
  4. Enter the IP address (192.168.x.x) below
